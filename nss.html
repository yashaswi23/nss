NSS EVENTS

Debate competition

NSS in association with the college organises various debate competitions for the students to bring out the inner confidence and to discover their hidden talents.



Poster-making competitions

We at NSS believe that everyone is equally talented but at the same time is diversified on the basis of field of talent.So we organise poster making competitions and other art related events so that one can truly discover his/her potential.



Organising social drives

Just as our motto depicts, we at NSS are determined to promote social awareness amongst the youth since they are the future of the country and the best asset to fight off these prevailant issues of the society, So we at college take the initiate and organise drives and camps to spread awareness and prepare the youth ie future of our country.



Cultural Events

In association with JCBoseUST, YMCA, NSS organises various cultural events as a part of the fest. We have nukkad nataks based off of some current social issue or dance performances or street plays and various other events to depict NSS and its need to the youth in an exciting and eye-grabbing way!



About us

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a Central Sector Scheme of Government of India, Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports. It provides opportunity to the student youth of 11th & 12th Class of schools at +2 Board level and student youth of Technical Institution, Graduate & Post Graduate at colleges and University level of India to take part in various government led community service activities & programmes.The sole aim of the NSS is to provide hands on experience to young students in delivering community service.
